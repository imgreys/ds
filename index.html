Game description and informations about the game
In Bacon May Die we play as a little pig again which fights through masses of enemies. Equipped with melee and ranged weapons we slash into them. Therefore we use the "arrow keys" left and right or the "finger". With a bit of skill and practice we do nice combos and use the ranged weapons. For enemies we have taken down we gain coins which can be used in the shops after the waves.
